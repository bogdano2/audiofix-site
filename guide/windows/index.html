Windows Audio Guides

Step-by-step guides to fix every common audio problem on Windows 10 and 11. Start with the core walkthrough, or jump straight to your specific issue.

Core walkthrough

Follow these three guides in order to understand and permanently fix audio device switching on Windows.

1

Find your devices

See every audio device Windows knows about. Identify your real speakers, headphones, and microphone. Spot the phantom devices causing trouble.

 2

Test your devices

Play a test tone through each output and record through each input to confirm which devices actually produce or capture sound.

 3

Lock it down

Set your correct defaults, disable unused devices, and configure app-level audio to prevent future drift.

Headphones not detected

Connect Bluetooth audio

Pair and configure Bluetooth headphones, speakers, or earbuds.

Bluetooth audio not working
